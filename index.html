What if we could enhance the learning experience beyond the Classroom?
What if your child learned about sustainable science by actually growing a plant?

Meet Bitpod

The BitPod is a growing container connected to the internet. It is armed with sensors that send data to a smartphone, and allows users to control the environment remotely. 

Control it from the App

Bitpod features a beautiful application that serves as a complete solution to your child's educational needs. With it they can control and monitor the plant, and view lessons to go along with the experiment.
